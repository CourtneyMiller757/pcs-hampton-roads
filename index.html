VA Loan Benefits

Make the Most of Your VA Benefit

Your VA loan benefit is one of the most powerful tools available to you. Here's what it can do when you work with an agent who knows how to use it.

  • No down payment required in most cases
  • No private mortgage insurance (PMI)
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Seller can pay all closing costs
  • No prepayment penalty
  • Reusable — use your benefit more than once
  • Assumable loan option for future buyers
  • Available for active duty, veterans, and surviving spouses
